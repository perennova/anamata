The foundation portfolio

Four operating companies. One thesis.

Each sovereign by design, each in a regulated, trust-sensitive domain, each built on the Keel.

01

Perennova

Revenue intelligence that rescues silent churn.

The AI layer on top of existing CRM and ERP. Perennova reads the signals revenue teams miss - accounts quietly disengaging, and latent expansion - and surfaces them while there is still time to act. Built on the Keel, so it ships in weeks: the clearest proof of the lab's repeatable-build model.

Revenue intelligence Lab-generated · the Forge Phase 2
Sovereignty posture

Runs within the customer's data boundary under the Keel's governance - no extractive use of customer data.

Enterprise cyber security

SOC 2-aligned controls with least-privilege, read-only access to CRM and ERP, encrypted in transit and at rest - and no customer data is ever used to train models.

02

Ambient Context

Turns energy waste into wealth - with nothing leaving the building.

An on-premises edge appliance, AmbientOS, that reads a site's electricity and orchestrates flexible load - HVAC, storage, EV and distributed energy - as part of a virtual power plant. The intelligence runs locally; data never leaves the site. Distribution is channel-led, so it scales capital-efficiently. Live today across Australia and Aotearoa New Zealand.

Energy · virtual power plant Direct investment Live · Australia & Aotearoa NZ
Sovereignty posture

On-premises edge compute with no data egress - procurement-grade for buyers who require data residency.

Enterprise cyber security

A hardened appliance with secure boot and signed firmware, segmented from operational technology and built to critical-infrastructure standards - it defends the site as well as reads it.

03

Forma Insights

Lab-grade movement intelligence, made accessible.

A movement-intelligence platform for athletes, coaches, teams and clinicians. Forma pairs wireless motion and body sensors, 4K video and AI to capture lab-grade movement data - gait, cadence, asymmetries, take-off and landing forces, and more - anywhere, not just in a lab. Developed with leading UK, Australian and New Zealand sports scientists, and delivered today across NZ and Australia through a national network of assessors.

Sports science · movement intelligence Direct investment Live · NZ & Australia
Sovereignty posture

Personal movement data handled under the NZ Privacy Act - built to be a trustworthy custodian of the athlete's data, not to harvest it.

Enterprise cyber security

Personal and biometric movement data encrypted end to end, with role-based clinician access and consent-gated sharing - independently penetration-tested to health-grade standards.

04

AKE

Wellbeing you can measure, owned by the people it serves.

An outcomes ledger governed by iwi (tribes and nations), paired with funding rails that move money to community-led delivery against real results. Aligned to Te Whare Tapa Whā - the holistic Māori model of wellbeing - and built to ride the Crown social-investment rails now opening. Success is measured for the mokopuna (future generations), not the quarter.

Social investment · wellbeing Direct investment Phase 1
Sovereignty posture

On-premises and iwi-controlled, no data egress - the community is the accountable owner of its own data.

Enterprise cyber security

A tamper-evident ledger with iwi-held encryption keys and community-controlled access - hardened on-premises and independently audited.

He karanga · a call for unity

Unity, from Aotearoa to the world.

In her first address as Māori Queen, Te Arikinui Kuīni Nga wai hono i te po called for kotahitanga - unity - “not just Māori”, and launched the Kotahitanga Fund, a Māori investment platform inviting global partners to build alongside iwi (tribes and nations) and whānau (families). Anamata answers that karanga, that call - and widens it: a kaupapa (shared purpose) for Indigenous peoples across the world, to unify, build and grow together. Capital that builds, rather than extracts.
